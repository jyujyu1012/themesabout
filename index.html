Co-Founder and CXO

Greg Lutze is the Co-Founder and Chief Experience Officer of VSCO, a photo company. VSCO has been named Apple’s “App of the Year,” Google Play’s “Best Apps” and Fast Company’s “Top 10 Most Innovative Companies in Social Media.” Before co-founding VSCO, Lutze worked as Creative Director for various agencies, with work being recognized by The Grammy Awards, Taschen, Communication Arts, Print Magazine and Graphic Design USA.
